# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 30, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the developer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the sorosoro application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

You can create an account in the App using one of the following methods:

- **Sign in with Apple**: Display name, email address (a relay address may be used via Apple's "Hide My Email" feature)
- **Sign in with Google**: Display name, email address

This information is used for authentication purposes through Firebase Authentication.

### 1.2 User-Provided Data

The following data is stored when you use the App's features:

- Routine information (name, icon, frequency, reminder settings, completion logs)
- Sharing settings (invitation IDs, shared usernames)

This data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) and shared among users who have established a connection via sharing settings.

### 1.3 Advertising Information

We use Google AdMob to deliver advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect the following information:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device information (OS version, device model, etc.)
- Information about ad views and clicks

On iOS, we display a request for tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you decline, personalized ads will not be delivered.

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect technical information through Firebase:

- Device type, OS version
- App version
- Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
- Performance data (Firebase Performance Monitoring)

---

## 2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To authenticate accounts and ensure security
- To provide routine management and notification features
- To synchronize data between shared users
- To deliver advertisements
- To improve the App and fix bugs

---

## 3. Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not provide your personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

- **Sharing with Other Users**: Users connected via invitation IDs can view each other's routine completion status.
- **Service Providers**: The App uses the following third-party services:
  - [Google Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) (Authentication and Database)
  - [Google AdMob](https://policies.google.com/privacy) (Ad Delivery)
- **Legal Requirements**: When disclosure is required by law or legal process

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- User data is stored on Google Firebase servers.
- All data transmission is encrypted using TLS.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account from the App's settings. Upon deletion, your user profile and all associated data will be removed from the server.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
